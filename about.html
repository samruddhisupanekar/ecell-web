About us

We at E-Cell RIT works as a start-up and help students in their
entrepreneurial journeys.
E-cell RIT provides great opportunities for start-ups, colleges,
and corporates to get involved with us.

To develop an ecosystem that will nurture the development of
the Entrepreneurial mindset of the RIT community;
which will be able to solve the problems profitably.


Misson

To provide efficient interaction between its major components spanning students,
working professionals,aspiringand existing entrepreneurs,
mentors, angel investors, venture capital firms,and corporate
through initiatives like interactive sessions.

To provide basic resources required for an enterprise.

To provide the necessary training programs for the E-Cell community.
